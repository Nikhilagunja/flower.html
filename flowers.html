FLOWERS

"flowers are the music of the ground,from earths lips spoken withount sound"

Flowers are one of nature’s most beautiful creations, admired for their vibrant colors, pleasant fragrances, and delicate shapes. Flowers not only add beauty to gardens and landscapes, but they also play a vital role in plant reproduction by attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. People often give flowers as gifts to express love, gratitude, or sympathy. Their presence brings joy and symbolizes the beauty and fragility of life.

TYPES OF FLOWERS

  • Rose
  • Tulip
  • Daisy
  • Sunflower
  • Lily
  • Orchid
